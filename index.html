About TrinkWink

Creating beautiful memories through thoughtful gifts

Our Story

At TrinkWink, we believe that every celebration deserves something special. From the sacred bond of rakhi to the joyous moments of weddings, we create beautiful, meaningful gifts that capture the essence of your special occasions.

Our handcrafted collection combines traditional elegance with modern aesthetics, ensuring that every piece tells a story and creates lasting memories. Based in Ahmedabad, we bring you the finest quality products with a personal touch.
